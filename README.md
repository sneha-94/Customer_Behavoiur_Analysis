# Customer_Behaviour_Analysis

## 📌 Overview

Customer Behaviour Analysis is a data analytics project focused on understanding customer shopping patterns, spending habits, and purchase trends. The project converts raw customer data into meaningful business insights using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

---

## 🚀 Tools & Technologies

* **Python**
* **Pandas / NumPy**
* **SQL (PostgreSQL)**
* **Power BI**
* **Jupyter Notebook**

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

* Customer segmentation based on previous purchases
* Category-wise sales analysis
* Subscription vs Non-subscription comparison
* Average purchase amount analysis
* Gender-wise customer insights
* Age group analysis
* Most purchased products by category
* Revenue trend analysis

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

* Total Customers
* Total Revenue
* Average Purchase Amount
* Subscription Breakdown
* Category-wise Sales
* Purchase Frequency Analysis
* Customer Segmentation

---

## 💡 Business Insights

This project helps businesses to:

* Identify loyal and returning customers
* Improve targeted marketing strategies
* Increase subscription conversions
* Optimize inventory planning
* Understand customer spending behavior
